Use active surfaces first:

- [README.md](README.md)
- [PARTS.md](PARTS.md)
- [parts/](parts/)

Open `legacy/` only for former path lookup, raw receipt intake, or distillation history. Former root file names stay historical here; active parts use their current route names.
