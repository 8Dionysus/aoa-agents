# Formation Trial Readiness

Formation readiness means the actor layer can be read by a future Agon protocol.

It does not mean the actor is already fighting.

## Pass criteria

The first Formation Trial passes only when:

- all five expected base profiles exist
- every expected role has a landed agonic form
- every expected role has a landed assistant variant
- at least one contestant candidate exists
- at least one witness candidate exists
- at least one judge candidate exists
- no closer candidate exists in this wave
- no summon initiator candidate exists in this wave
- no assistant variant is arena eligible
- generated output is deterministic

The desired first pass is disciplined incompleteness: enough actors to proceed, not enough authority to secretly open the arena.
