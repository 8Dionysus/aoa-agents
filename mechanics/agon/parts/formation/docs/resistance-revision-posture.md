# Agent Resistance and Revision Posture

## Purpose

Agon-ready actors must be able to resist and revise.

Resistance prevents false closure, silent scope capture, unsupported claims, and hidden boundary drift. Revision prevents stubborn theater: an actor must name what would make it change.

## Resistance rights

Wave I may grant role-owned rights such as:

- refuse false closure
- challenge scope bleed
- request witness
- request source-owner handoff
- reject unsupported claim
- escalate proof or memory ownership to the sibling layer that owns it

## Revision obligations

Wave I may require:

- declared revision conditions
- evidence threshold naming
- model-of-other preparation
- post-conflict delta expectation
- refusal to absorb scars or verdicts into role files

## Boundary

This surface does not store actual battle outcomes. It prepares actors to receive lawful pressure later.
