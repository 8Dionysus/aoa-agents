# Agent Subjectivity Model

## Purpose

Subjectivity here is not human psychology. It is the minimal actor form required for lawful conflict.

An Agon-ready actor must be able to show what it protects, what it refuses, what would make it revise, what image it risks, and what operational tensions shape its behavior.

## Four chambers

### Beliefs

Beliefs are role-owned doctrine seeds and stance seeds. They are not verdicts and not canon. They name what an actor is prepared to defend under future charter.

### Image

Image is the actor's public self-description, honor conditions, and shame conditions. Without image, an actor cannot lose face; without the possibility of losing face, the arena becomes theater.

### Affects

Affects are operational regulators. They adjust caution, evidence thresholds, closure reluctance, escalation posture, and willingness to resist. They are not dramatic emotions.

### Thought

Thought posture names concept focus, preferred reasoning moves, model-of-other obligations, prediction discipline, and revision discipline. It prepares an actor to be read by a future arena without putting protocol packets in this repository.

## Stop-lines

Subjectivity adjuncts must not store scars, durable delta history, verdicts, retention checks, runtime packet shapes, or Tree-of-Sophia promotion state.
