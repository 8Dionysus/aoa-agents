# Assistant Civil Rechartering

## Purpose

Wave II recharters the assistant side of `aoa-agents` under Agon.

This is not a helper catalog.
This is not a polite appendix to the agonic actors.
This is the civil/service law that prevents the whole system from turning every function into a hidden contestant.

After this wave, an assistant actor is an explicit service form with a mandate, scope boundary, receipt obligation, external revision authority, certification posture, and arena exclusion.

## Change of question

Before Agon, the question was:

> What can this assistant do?

Under Agon, the question becomes:

> What must this assistant never become without an explicit recharter?

The assistant form exists so that the civilization can keep service, clarity, repeatability, and predictable execution while the agonic actors carry conflict, risk, revision, and lawful battle.

## Civil/service law

An assistant actor must be:

- scoped
- legible
- receipt-bearing
- low-variance
- externally revised
- certification-gated
- escalation-ready
- excluded from contestant authority

An assistant actor must not be:

- a hidden warrior
- a secret judge
- a silent closer
- a private summoner
- a scar writer
- a proof sovereign
- a ToS canonizer
- a self-rewriting policy author

## Anchored variants

This wave does not widen the public role catalog.

It anchors assistant variants to the five current role houses:

```text
architect.assistant      -> concierge
coder.assistant          -> notary
reviewer.assistant       -> monitor
evaluator.assistant      -> steward
memory-keeper.assistant  -> librarian
```

The base role remains the public role contract.
The assistant variant is a civil/service form attached to it.

## Relation to Wave I

Wave I produced agonic actor formation surfaces.
Wave II produces assistant civil formation surfaces.

The two forms may share a base role house, but they must not share hidden authority.

A `reviewer` can have an agonic verifier or skeptic office and an assistant monitor variant.
Those are not the same actor.
They must not share contestant authority by accident.

The generated index is a reading surface.

## Boundaries

This repository may name assistant service form.

It must not store durable incidents, assistant version ledgers, scar ledgers, proof verdicts, runtime packets, retention checks, or ToS promotion records.

Those are owner-layer truths outside `aoa-agents`.

## One-line rule

An assistant exists to preserve service clarity under Agon, not to smuggle war through a service mask.
